====== worship ======

**敬拜**
===== Definition: =====

定義：

"To worship" means to honor, praise and obey someone, especially God.

敬拜是表示向某人致敬，讚美和順服，特別是向神。

  * We worship God when we serve and honor him, by praising him and obeying him.
  * 當我們藉著讚美和順服來事奉他和尊敬他的時候，那就是敬拜神。
  * When the Israelites worshiped God, it often included sacrificing an animal on an altar.
  * 當以色列人敬拜神的時候，通常會在祭壇上獻上一隻動物。
  * Some people worship false gods.
  * 有些人敬拜假神。

[See also: **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:key-terms:sacrifice|sacrifice]]**, **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:key-terms:praise|praise]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

出於聖經故事的例子：

  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:13-04|[13-04]]]** Then God gave them the covenant and said, "I am Yahweh, your God, who saved you from slavery in Egypt. Do not **worship**  other gods.”
  * 然後神給他們立約說，"我是耶和華，你的神，曾把你從埃及地拯救出來。你不可敬拜別神”。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:14-02|[14-02]]]** The Canaanites did not **worship**  or obey God. They **worshiped**  false gods and did many evil things.
  * 那些迦南人即不敬拜神也不順服神。但他們卻去拜一些假神並且作了許多的惡事。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:17-06|[17-06]]]** David wanted to build a temple where all the Israelites could **worship**  God and offer him sacrifices.
  * 大衛想要為神建一座聖殿，好讓所有的以色列人都能夠在殿裡敬拜神，向神供奉祭品。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:18-12|[18-12]]]** All of the kings and most of the people of the kingdom of Israel **worshiped**  idols.
  * 所有的以色列國的君王和其大部分的人民都敬拜偶像。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:25-07|[25-07]]]** Jesus replied, "Get away from me, Satan! In God's word he commands his people, '**Worship**  only the Lord your God and only serve him.'"
  *
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:26-02|[26-02]]]** On the Sabbath, he (Jesus) went to the place of **worship**.
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:47-01|[47-01]]]** There they met a woman named Lydia who was a merchant. She loved and **worshiped**  God.
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:49-18|[49-18]]]** God tells you to pray, to study his word, to **worship**  him with other Christians, and to tell others what he has done for you.
