====== Jacob雅各 ======

雅各

===== Facts: =====

定義：

Jacob was the second of the twin sons that were born to Isaac and Rebekah. The first twin born was Esau.

雅各是以撒和利百加所生的双生子的次子。以掃是這双生子的長子。

  * Jacob's name means "deceiver" or "trickster."
  * 雅各的名字翻譯出來就是欺騙者或詐欺者。
  * Jacob was clever and deceptive. He found ways to take both the firstborn inheritance rights and the blessing from his older brother, Esau.
  * 雅各即聰明又虛假。他找到可以從他哥哥，以掃，那裡騙取長子繼承權和祝福的方法。
  * Esau was angry and planned to kill him, so Jacob left his homeland. But he later returned and lived peacefully with his brother.
  * 以掃相當生氣，打算殺了他，可是雅各已經從他的家鄉逃走了。不過，後來他卻又回到家鄉與他的哥哥和平的居住。
  * God changes Jacob's name to Israel, which means, "He struggles with God."
  * 神把雅各的名字改為以撒，它的意思是，“雅各與神博鬥”。
  * God kept his covenant to Abraham and his descendants through Abraham's son, Isaac, and Isaac's son, Jacob.
  * 神持守他與亞伯拉罕和他的子孫從亞伯拉罕的兒子，以撒，到以撒的兒子，雅各所立的約。
  * Jacob had twelve sons. Their descendants became the twelve tribes of Israel.
  * 雅各有十二個兒子。他們的後裔後來成為以色列的十二個支派。

【參見：以撒，利百加，以掃，以色列】

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

聖經故事裡的例子：

  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:07-01|[07-01]]]** As the boys grew up, Rebekah loved **Jacob**, but Isaac loved Esau. **Jacob**  loved to stay at home, but Esau loved to hunt.
  * 當孩子們都長大了，利百加專愛雅各，以撒卻愛以掃。雅各喜歡待在家裡，而以掃卻喜歡守獵。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:07-07|[07-07]]]****Jacob**  lived there for many years, and during that time he married and had twelve sons and a daughter. God made him very wealthy.
  * 雅各在那裡住了許多年。當時，他結了婚並在婚後生了十二個兒子和一個女兒。神使他很富有。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:07-08|[07-08]]]** After twenty years away from his home in Canaan, **Jacob**  returned there with his family, his servants, and all his herds of animals.
  * 在離開他在迦南的家二十年之後，雅各帶著他的家人，僕人們，以及他所有的家畜一起又回到那裡。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:07-10|[07-10]]]** The covenant promises God had promised to Abraham and then to Isaac now passed on to **Jacob**.
  * 神應許亞伯拉罕所立的約傳給了以撒之後，現在又傳給雅各了。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:08-01|[08-01]]]** Many years later, when **Jacob**  was an old man, he sent his favorite son, Joseph, to check on his brothers who were taking care of the herds.
  * 許多年後，當雅各年老時，他打發他所愛的兒子，約瑟，去查看他哥哥們牧放羊群的情形。
