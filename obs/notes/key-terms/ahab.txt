====== Ahab 雅哈 ======

===== Facts: 事例 =====

Ahab was a very evil king who ruled over the northern kingdom of Israel.

亚哈是一个非常邪恶的君王， 他统制着以色列的北部王国。

  * King Ahab was wicked and encouraged the people of the kingdom of Israel to worship false gods.
  * 亚哈王非常邪恶，并怂恿以色列的人民敬拜假神。
  * The prophet Elijah confronted Ahab and told him that there would be a severe drought for three and a half years as a punishment for the sins that Ahab caused Israel to commit.
  * 先知以利亚面对亚哈，告诉他将有三年半的严重旱灾，作为对引导以色列犯下的罪行的惩罚。
  * Ahab had 450 prophets to the false god named Baal. Elijah challenged those prophets and proved that Yahweh is the only true God.
  * 亚哈有450个都信一个名叫巴力的假神的先知。以利亚向这些先知提出挑战，证明耶和华是唯一的真神。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:19-02|[19-02]]]** Elijah was a prophet when **Ahab** was king over the kingdom of Israel. **Ahab** was an evil man who encouraged people to worship a false god named Baal.
  ***[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:19-03|[19-03]]]** **Ahab** and his army looked for Elijah, but they could not find him.
  ***[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:19-05|[19-05]]]** After three and a half years, God told Elijah to return to the kingdom of Israel and speak with **Ahab** because he was going to send rain again.
