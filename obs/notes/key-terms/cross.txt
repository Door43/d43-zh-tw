====== cross ======

十字架

===== Definition： =====

定义：

A cross is an upright wooden post stuck into the ground, with a horizontal wooden beam attached to it near the top.

十字架是一块竖起的木头钉在地里，在靠木头的上端钉上一块横木而形成十字形。

  * The Roman government would tie or nail criminals onto crosses and leave them there to die.
  * 罗马帝国将罪犯用钉子钉或绑在十字架上直到他们死亡。
  * Jesus was falsely accused and judged to be guilty of crimes he did not commit. The Romans killed him on a cross.
  * 主耶稣无辜未曾犯任何罪却被判刑，被罗马统治者钉死在十字架上。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

来自圣经故事实例：

  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:40-01|[40-01]]]** After the soldiers mocked Jesus, they led him away to crucify him. They made him carry the **cross**  on which he would die.
  * 士兵们先是讥笑主耶稣，然后他们让主耶稣背负着十字架，主耶稣将会被钉死在这个十字架上。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:40-02|[40-02]]]** The soldiers brought Jesus to a place called "the Skull" and nailed his arms and feet to the **cross**.
  * 士兵们带主耶稣来到了一个叫“骷髅地”的地方，将他 的双手和双脚的钉在十字架上。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:40-05|[40-05]]]** The Jewish leaders and the other people in the crowd mocked Jesus. They said to him, "If you are the Son of God, come down from the **cross**  and save yourself! Then we will believe you."
  * 犹太人 领袖和一些民众讥笑主耶稣，他们对主耶稣说：“如果你是神的儿子，你就从十字架上下来，解救自己！那样我们就相信你。”
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:49-10|[49-10]]]** When Jesus died on the **cross**, he received your punishment.
  * 当主耶稣被钉死在十字架上，他就替代你而受惩罚。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:49-12|[49-12]]]** You must believe that Jesus is the Son of God, that he died on the **cross**  instead of you, and that God raised him to life again.
  * 你必须相信耶稣是上帝的儿子，他在十字 架上替你受死，上帝让他死而复活上天堂。
