====== word of God ======

**神的話**
==== (word of God, God's word, scripture, Word of God) ====

**（神的話，神的話語，聖經，神的道）**
===== Definition: =====

**定義：**

The "word of God" refers to all that God has said to people. Usually it refers to his written message, also known as the "scriptures."

“神的話”是指神對人所說過的話。它一般又指凡有文字記載下來的他的信息，它又稱為“聖經”。

  * God used prophets, apostles, and other people to communicate the "word of God" to us.
  * 神藉著眾先知，眾使徒及其他的人把神的話傳達給我們。
  * In modern times, the "word of God" refers to the Bible.
  * 在現今的世代裡，“神的話”就是指著聖經。
  * Some languages will need to translate "God's word" as, "God's message" or, "God's book" or, "God's scroll."
  * 某些語言是要用來把“神的話語”翻譯成冊的，如“神的道理”或“神的書”或“神的書卷”等等。
  * Jesus is also called, "the Word" and, "the Word of God." These titles mean that he fully reveals who God is. He can do this because he is God himself.
  * 耶穌也曾被稱呼為“道”和“神的道”。這些稱呼是表示只有他能夠把神是誰完整地啟示出來，因為他自己就是神自己。

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

**出於聖經故事的例子：**

  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:25-07|[25-07]]]** In **God's word**  he commands his people, 'Worship only the Lord your God and only serve him.'"
  * 他以**神的話****語**  來分附他的人民，“當拜主你的神，單要事奉他”。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:33-06|[33-06]]]** So Jesus explained, "The seed is the **word of God**.
  * 耶穌解釋說，“這種子就是**神的話**”。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:42-03|[42-03]]]** Then Jesus explained to them what **God's word**  says about the Messiah.
  * 耶穌向他們講解所有的有關的**神的話語**  都是指著彌賽亞說的
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:42-07|[42-07]]]** Jesus said, "I told you that everything written about me in **God's word**  must be fulfilled." Then he opened their minds so they could understand **God's word**.
  * 耶穌說，“我告訴你們凡在**神的話語**  中所有寫著有關我的事都要應驗。” 然後他開他們的心竅，使他們明白**神的話語**  。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:45-10|[45-10]]]** Philip also used other **scriptures**  to tell him the good news of Jesus.
  * 腓利還引用別的**經文**  來向他傳講耶穌的福音。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:48-12|[48-12]]]** But Jesus is the greatest prophet of all. He is the **Word of God**.
  * 耶穌是所有的先知中最偉大的先知。他也是**神的道**  。
  * **[[:zh-tw:obs:notes:frames:49-18|[49-18]]]** God tells you to pray, to study his **word**, to worship him with other Christians, and to tell others what he has done for you.
  * 神已經吩咐你要去禱告，要去察考他的道，要與其他的基督徒一起來敬拜，也要告訴其他人他已經為你做了些甚麼。
